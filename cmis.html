Overview

Worked with the Ministry of Health Strategic Information Department (MoH SID) to developed and maintained a comprehensive Electronic Medical Record (EMR) system designed to streamline patient data management, optimize clinical workflows, and improve continuity of care for Government Health Facilities in Eswatini. The system serves as a central hub for securely storing, retrieving, and processing patient records, supporting a wide range of medical operations, including screening, diagnosis, labs, prescription management, appointment scheduling and more. To enhance the system's scalability, performance, and interoperability, I contributed to the design and implementation of multiple modules and services, handling functionalities such as clinical notifications, electronic lab request transfer, and other integrations. These services were architected to be highly available, fault-tolerant, and compliant with industry standards like HIPAA and HL7/FHIR.

Key Contributions

  • FHIR compliance - Ensured the CMIS is FHIR compliant by implementing functionality that transforms CMIS data from basic structure to FHIR resources such as the Patient Resource for patient demographics, Diagnostic Report for lab requests, Observation for clinical observations Medication Request for prescriptions and more. I also implemented FHIR-based APIs to facilitate interoperability between the CMIS and Health Information Exchange.
  • Module Development - Designed and developed a number of role based modules for managing patient's clinical information such as Labs, Prescriptions, TB Screening Vitals and more.
  • Integration - Integrated the CMIS with other systems such as the Laboratory Information System (LIS) using standards such as HL7 v2 to ensure real-time and paper less lab requesting and results process.
  • Performance Optimization - Optimized system query execution to ensure fast retrieval of data across different modules. Implemented caching strategies to improve response time for frequently accessed modules.
  • Security - Ensure enryption of sensitive data such as PII safeguarding data agains potential breaches and unauthorized access. Impletened API security for all exposed CMIS APIs. Performed regular security audits such as penetration testing to identify system vulnerabilities and develop patches.
