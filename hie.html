Overview

Worked with the Ministry of Health Strategic Information Department (MoH SID) to developed a standards based integration framework aimed at harmonizing disparate health information systems and streamline data exchange across the healthcare ecosystem.

Key Contributions

  • Standards-Based Integration - Developed an interoperability framework based on FHIR (Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resource) HL7 and OpenHIE standards to enable seamless communication between electronic health information systems and electronic data collection tools.
  • Master Patient Index - Developed a centralized Master Patient Index (MPI) to uniquely identify and tack patients across multiple healthcare systems, reducing duplicate records and ensuring accurate longitudinal patient history. This laveraged probabilistic and deterministic matching algorithms to link records from different systems while ensuring patient confidentiality.
  • Shared Health Record - Developed a FHIR based Shared Health Record (SHR) to store patient data from disparate sources, allowing healthcare providers to access a unified patient history for better clinical decision making.
  • Data Exchange Gateway - Led the deployment of a data exchange gateway that enabled different systems to submit and retrieve patient data securely.
  • Capacity Building & Knowledge Transfer - Worked alongside the development team to train stakeholders on the use of the integration framework and the FHIR standard enabling seamless integration of other systems into the framework.
