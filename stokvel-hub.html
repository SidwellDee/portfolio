Overview

To enhance the efficiency and transparency of stokvel (group savings) management I developed the Stokvel HUB system, a digital platform designed to facilitate the registration, finacial tracking and management of stokvel groups. The system enables stokvel members to record and monitor their contributions, borrowings and group balances in real time, ensuring fairness, accountability and ease of finacial management. The Stokvel HUB provedes an intuitive interface that allows groups to digitally manage monthly contributions, loan disbursements, repayment tracking and finacial reporting. This system reduces administrative burdens, improves financial transparency and ensures that group members have full visibility into their finacial activities.

Key Contributions

  • Stokvel Group Registration & Membership Management - Developed a registration module to allow stokvel groups to digitally register and onboard members with verified identity details. In addition I implemented role based access control enabling groups to assign key roles within their respective groups.
  • Monthly Contribution & Fund Tracking - Designed and develped module to record member deposits, validate payments and updates group balances in realtime.
  • Borrowing & Loan Management - Implemented a stokvel lendimg module that allows groups to issue loans to members, track borrowings, and calculate interest.
  • Finacial Dashboard & Balance Monitoring - Developed an interactive finacial dashboard displaying total group savings, monthly contributions and loan disbursements.
  • Security & Data Integrity - Developed multi-level authentication to enhance security for finacial transactions.
