Overview

To improve the monitoring and management of People Living with HIV (PLHIVs), I developed a comprehensive Data Collection Tool designed to track adherence, ART regimen changes, Community ART Groups (CAGs), commodity distribution, support group participation and treatment literacy. The tools serves as a digital platform to collect, analyze and report key patient data related to HIV care and treatment. This tool enables real-time data capture and automated reporting ensuring that PLHIVs receive continuos support, timely interventions and improved treatment outcomes.

Key Contributions

  • ART Regimen & Adherence Tracking - Implemented tracking of ART Regimen allowing healthcare providers to document regimen changes, side effects and and adherence challenges over time.
  • Community ART Groups (CAGs) and Support Groups Management - Developed a Community ART Groups tracking feature to monitor group enrollments, meeting attendance and ART refills within decentralized care models. Developed a Support Group Management feature that allows healthcare workers to track PLHIV participatio in psychosocial support programs and treatment literacy initiative.
  • Commodity Distribution - Designed a commodity distribution module to track ART kit disbursement.
  • Automated Reports - Developed automated reporting templates to enable healthcare workers assess the effectiveness of HIV interventsions.
